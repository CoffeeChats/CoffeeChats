Our Mission

We hope to give back to the community by providing a resource for students to help them understand the professional world and aid them in landing jobs. Our team of mentors have all successfully landed positions at top consulting firms and are eager to share what we have learned along the way.

Career Coaching

We will work with students to better define the different avenues within consulting that they can pursue. Working with their interests to help find a direction to target.

Resume Review

We will provide free resume reviews as well as offer templates and edits.

Interview Preparation

We will walkthrough the interview process as well as utilize a collection of past interview materials to help prepare mentees.

Our Process

So how do we start?

  • Step 1

    Reach Out

    Send us an email through our website, located at the bottom of the page, to kick off our matching process.

  • Step 2

    Fill Out Our Survey

    Once we receive your email, we will reach out with a survey to gather more information about how we can help. Whether the focus is needed on resume improvement, behavioral interview prep, or just general career coaching, we'd be happy to help.

  • Step 3

    Keep An Eye Out

    Our team will review the survey response and send an introductory email to you and your mentor.

  • Step 4

    Chat!

    At this stage email your mentor and find a time to chat. Once a date and time is set, connect!
